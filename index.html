ChefMeet

Bootcamp Project | 3 weeks | Group (5 people)

Nowadays, people are too busy to make home-cooked meals in their households, so we decided to create an application that gives the user the convenience and variety of culturally diverse home-cooked meals, made fresh right in the safety of their own home. ChefMeet is an application that allows busy individuals, families, and people who are throwing parties to connect with a personal chef to meet their needs. The project’s purpose was to diminish unhealthy options that people choose, instead of cooking home-cooked meals due to their busy lifestyle. We wanted to create a way for people to have an active, healthy option while having the food fresh and ready for the individual(s) when desired.

Honest Beauty

Bootcamp Project | 2 weeks | Partner

Honest is a wellness brand empowering people to live happy, healthy lives. The company promises healthy natural ingredients in all products. Our goal was to redesign the website and add new features to the site, making it easier for the users to find the perfect foundation match for their skin tone. We made the journey a customized user friendly experience. The user can fill out their answers to a quiz, which generates the perfect match for their foundation shade, it also suggests all the different products that give you the finish you are looking for and works well with the skin problems you might have.
